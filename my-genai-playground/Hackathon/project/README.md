Career development sirf technical skills ka naam nahi hai, balkay ye sahi strategy aur ek "Warrior Mindset" ka imtiaz hai. **Muhammad Bilal** ne AuraPath AI ko isliye design kiya taake career roadmap ko data-driven banaya ja sakay aur user ko rejections ke khilaf mentally prepare kiya ja sakay, bilkul unhi asoolon par jin par Thomas Edison ne dunya ko roshan kiya.

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture
### 1. Multi-Modal Intelligence (The Ears)
* **Whisper-V3 Integration:** System 99+ languages ko support karta hai, jis mein English, Urdu, aur Roman-Urdu shamil hain.
* **Voice-First Design:** User apni awaaz mein commands de sakta hai, jo system ko mazeed accessible banata hai.

### 2. Agentic Workflow (The Brain)
* **NEXUS (Technical Audit):** User ki CV ka exhaustive audit karta hai taake skill gaps identify ho saken.
* **PATHFINDER (Market Scout):** Profile ko scan kar ke un industries ko pinpoint karta hai jahan hiring probability highest hai.
* **ZEN (Resilience Engine):** Context-aware motivation deliver karta hai taake candidate ka morale kabhi down na ho.
