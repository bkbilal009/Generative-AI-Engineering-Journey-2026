## 🏗️ Technical Architecture

### 1. Multi-Lingual Voice Intelligence
*   **Whisper-V3 Integration:** Supports 99+ languages, including **English, Urdu, and Roman-Urdu**.
*   **Speech-to-Text:** High-precision voice recognition using **Whisper-large-v3**.

### 2. The Agentic Workflow
*   **NEXUS (Technical Audit):** Performs a deep-dive technical audit of your CV to identify skill gaps.
*   **PATHFINDER (Market Scout):** Scans industries to identify companies where your hiring probability is highest.
*   **ZEN (Warrior Mindset):** Delivers high-intensity motivation based on historical legends to build mental resilience.
*   **ORBIT (The Strategist):** Generates a comprehensive 6-month tactical execution plan.
